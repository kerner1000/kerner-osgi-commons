Preparing your workspace to develop new Anna-Steps

To develop new steps, you need to import Anna core Plug-Ins and also it's dependencies to your workspace.

  • Download Anna

    Download

    anna-core-v.0.0.2.zip

    and extract it to a location of your choice.

  • Import Plug-Ins

    Copy all jar-files contained in folders 01-* till 05-* into the plugins-folder of your Equinox-SDK installation.

    For example

    /home/alex/equinox-SDK-3.4/eclipse/plugins

  • Reload Target Platform

    In order to make this imports take effect, you have to reload your Target Platform. To do so, go to Window → Preferences, select Plug-in Development → Target Platform, select your Target Platform and click Reload...

  • Done!

    That's it, you are now able to use Anna-classes from within your own Eclipse-Plug-In Project.

    Continue with Creating a new step.
